EduBridge

“Connecting Classrooms. Empowering Futures.”

🚨 What Problem Are We Solving?

Over 60% of children in rural regions lack access to quality education due to poor infrastructure, lack of qualified teachers, and limited digital resources.

💡 Our Product/Service

EduBridge is a solar-powered, internet-enabled digital classroom kit that brings quality educational content to rural schools. Each kit includes:

  • A tablet preloaded with the national curriculum
  • Teacher training materials
  • Offline access to recorded lectures
  • A portable solar panel for power

🎯 Our Target Customers

We serve:

  1. Non-profit and government schools in rural and underdeveloped areas
  2. Education-focused NGOs
  3. CSR departments of large corporations

💰 Revenue Model

We will generate revenue through:

  • Direct sales of kits to NGOs and schools
  • Subscription-based content updates and remote support
  • Partnerships with government education programs
